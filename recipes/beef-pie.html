Beef Pie

A warm and hearty classic for the ages.

This beef pie will thaw any frozen heart on the coldest day of the year.

With plenty of vegetables and gravy, this dish will fill you up and revitalize you.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Beef
  • Broth
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Make your crust from the flour, butter, milk and some salt.
  2. Put some butter in a pan and saute your steak.
  3. Once done, add broth and more flour to the steak to make your gravy.
  4. Cook veggies with your gravy and steak.
  5. Put together the dough and mixture and bake it up!
