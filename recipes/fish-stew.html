Fish Stew

An aroma to warm you from inside out.

This fish stew is an intriguing flavor for sure.

There is a spice and flavor unmatched by western cooking.

Ingredients

  • Fish
  • Dashi
  • Spices
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Clean ingredients.
  2. Put all ingredients together in a pot.
  3. Bring to a boil, then let simmer for as long as you'd like. The long, the better.
